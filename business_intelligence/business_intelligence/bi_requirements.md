# Business Intelligence Requirements

## Purpose
The purpose of Business Intelligence in the USH BRIDGE Consults database system
is to support management in monitoring projects, revenue, and system security.

## Stakeholders
- Company management
- Project managers
- Finance team
- System administrators

## Decision Support Needs
- Monitor project performance and completion status
- Track revenue generated per project and per client
- Identify high-value clients
- Monitor denied and allowed database actions for security

## Key Data Sources
- Projects table
- Sales table
- Clients table
- Audit_log table

## Reporting Frequency
- Project and revenue reports: Monthly
- Audit and security reports: Weekly
- Management summaries: Quarterly
